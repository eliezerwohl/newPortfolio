About Me

I’ve been working on websites for many years, since the days of Homestead, Xanga, and GeoCities. I’ve always had a desire to do more with this hobby.


In 2015, I saw an ad for a Rutgers Coding Bootcamp, and I was immediately intrigued. I was impressed by the JavaScript based full stack curriculum. Ever since I stepped into the first class, I’ve been hooked. I’ve been coding and improving my skills every day.


Since finishing the course, I've been working as a freelance Web Developer. I do everything from setting up hosting, to building websites from scratch, to troubleshooting issues. I work with clients to help achieve their online vision, and do contract work for companies to resolve WordPress or JavaScript related issues.
